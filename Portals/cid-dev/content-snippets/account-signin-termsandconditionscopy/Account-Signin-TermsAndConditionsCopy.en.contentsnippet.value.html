Access to this system is restricted to authorized individuals only.

Transport Canada reserves the right to monitor all electronic resources and subsequently record all forms of communication and transmission for work-related purposes to ensure proper network performance and security, protection of government assets, optimal use of network resources and compliance with relevant legislation and policies. Monitoring to gather information to investigate and resolve suspected cases of unacceptable use may occur at any time without further notification.

Unlawful or unacceptable activities as outlined in the Treasury Board Policy on Service and Digital, while using Transport Canada's electronic networks or devices is not permitted. Failure to abide by Treasury Board and/or TC policies and guidelines may be considered a security incident, and will be referred to Departmental authorities for further investigation.
